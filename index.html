About

I work at the intersection of policy, communications, and technical problem-solving. Based in NYC, I'm interested in how systems work — how cities are built, how institutions operate, how stories get told, and how technology can serve public interest.

My background is in public policy and journalism. I've worked on elections and campaigns, managed social impact initiatives, rebuilt a family business, and spent time in the U.S. Senate. I'm equally comfortable writing policy briefs, designing e-commerce systems, and thinking about the infrastructure that holds cities together.
